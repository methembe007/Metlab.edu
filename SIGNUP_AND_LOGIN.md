# 📚 Metlab.edu - Signup and Login Guide

Welcome to Metlab.edu! This guide will walk you through the process of creating an account and logging into our AI-powered learning platform.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Step 1: Access the Platform
1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to: `https://metlab.edu` (or your local development URL)
3. You'll see the homepage with login and signup options

---

## 📝 Creating Your Account (Signup Process)

### Step 1: Navigate to Registration
1. Click the **"Sign Up"** or **"Create Account"** button on the homepage
2. You'll be redirected to the registration page

### Step 2: Choose Your Account Type
Metlab.edu supports three types of users:

#### 🎓 **Student Account**
- For learners who want to access AI-powered educational content
- Upload study materials and get personalized learning experiences
- Track progress with gamification features
- Join study groups and connect with peers

#### 👨‍🏫 **Teacher Account**
- For educators who want to manage classes and students
- Create and assign educational content
- Monitor student progress and analytics
- Manage classroom activities and assessments

#### 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 **Parent Account**
- For parents who want to monitor their children's learning
- View child's progress and achievements
- Set screen time limits and learning goals
- Receive progress reports and notifications

### Step 3: Fill Out Registration Form

#### Required Information:
1. **First Name**: Your first name
2. **Last Name**: Your last name
3. **Username**: Choose a unique username (3-150 characters)
4. **Email Address**: Valid email address for account verification
5. **Account Type**: Select Student, Teacher, or Parent
6. **Institution**: (Required for Teachers only) Your school or educational institution
7. **Password**: Strong password meeting security requirements
8. **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password to confirm

#### Password Requirements:
- At least 8 characters long
- Cannot be too similar to your personal information
- Cannot be a commonly used password
- Cannot be entirely numeric

### Step 4: Submit Registration
1. Review all information for accuracy
2. Click **"Create Account"** button
3. You'll see a success message: *"Registration successful! Please check your email to verify your account."*

### Step 5: Email Verification
1. Check your email inbox (and spam folder)
2. Look for an email from Metlab.edu with subject "Verify Your Account"
3. Click the verification link in the email
4. Your account will be activated and ready to use

---

## 🔐 Logging Into Your Account

### Step 1: Access Login Page
1. Go to the Metlab.edu homepage
2. Click **"Login"** or **"Sign In"**
3. You'll be redirected to the login page

### Step 2: Enter Credentials
1. **Username**: Enter your username (not email)
2. **Password**: Enter your password
3. Click **"Sign In"** button

### Step 3: Account Verification Check
- If your email isn't verified, you'll see: *"Please verify your email address before logging in."*
- Check your email and click the verification link first

### Step 4: Successful Login
Upon successful login, you'll be redirected to your personalized dashboard based on your account type:

#### Student Dashboard Features:
- 📊 Learning progress overview
- 📚 Content library access
- 🎮 Gamification elements (XP, achievements, streaks)
- 👥 Study groups and community features
- 📈 Personal analytics

#### Teacher Dashboard Features:
- 👨‍🎓 Class management
- 📋 Student progress monitoring
- 📝 Content creation and assignment tools
- 📊 Classroom analytics
- 🎯 Assessment tools

#### Parent Dashboard Features:
- 👶 Children's progress overview
- ⏰ Screen time monitoring
- 🎯 Learning goal tracking
- 📧 Progress notifications
- 🔒 Privacy and safety controls

## 🔧 Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Registration Problems

#### "Username already exists"
- Try a different username
- Usernames must be unique across the platform

#### "Email already registered"
- You may already have an account
- Try logging in instead
- Use the "Forgot Password" feature if needed

#### "Password too weak"
- Ensure password meets all requirements
- Use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
- Avoid common passwords

### Login Problems

#### "Invalid username or password"
- Double-check your username (not email)
- Ensure caps lock is off
- Try typing password manually instead of copy-paste

#### "Please verify your email address"
- Check your email inbox and spam folder
- Click the verification link
- Request a new verification email if needed

#### "Account locked" or Rate Limiting
- Wait 5 minutes before trying again
- The system allows 5 login attempts per minute for security

### Email Verification Issues

#### "Didn't receive verification email"
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Ensure email address was entered correctly
3. Contact support for manual verification

#### "Verification link expired"
1. Request a new verification email
2. Links expire after 24 hours for security
