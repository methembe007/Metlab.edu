# PDF Viewing Quick Start Guide

## For Teachers

### How to Upload a PDF

1. **Login** to your teacher account
2. Navigate to **Teacher Dashboard** or click **Upload Content** in the menu
3. Click **Upload Content** button
4. Fill in the form:
   - **File**: Select your PDF file (max 50MB)
   - **Title**: Give it a descriptive title
   - **Subject**: Select the subject area
   - **Description**: Add details about the content
   - **Assign to Classes**: Select which classes should have access
5. Click **Upload**
6. Wait for AI processing to complete (generates summaries, quizzes, flashcards)

### Managing Uploaded PDFs

- View all your content at: `/learning/teacher/content/`
- Click on any content to see details and generated materials
- Assign/unassign from classes as needed
- Track which students have accessed the content

## For Students

### How to View PDFs

#### Method 1: Through Your Classes
1. **Login** to your student account
2. Go to **My Classes**
3. Click on a class
4. Browse the assigned content
5. Click **View PDF** button on any PDF

#### Method 2: All Content View
1. Go to **All Content** (shows content from all your classes)
2. Use search/filters to find specific PDFs
3. Click **View Details** or **View PDF**

#### Method 3: Direct Access
1. If you have a direct link to content, click it
2. You'll see the content detail page with:
   - Embedded PDF viewer
   - Download button
   - AI-generated study materials

### Viewing Options

**Embedded Viewer**
- PDFs display directly in the content detail page
- 600px viewing area
- Scroll to read the entire document

**New Tab Viewer**
- Click "View PDF" button
- Opens in new browser tab
- Full-screen viewing experience
- Use browser's PDF controls (zoom, print, etc.)

**Download**
- Click "Download" button
- Saves PDF to your device
- Study offline

### Study Features

When viewing a PDF, you also get access to:
- **Summaries**: Quick overview of the content
- **Quizzes**: Test your understanding
- **Flashcards**: Review key concepts

## Troubleshooting

### "You don't have access to this content"
- Make sure you're enrolled in the class
- Contact your teacher to assign the content to your class

### PDF not loading
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Try the "Open in new tab" option
- Contact support if issue persists

### Cannot download PDF
- Check browser download settings
- Ensure pop-ups are not blocked
- Try a different browser

## Tips

### For Teachers
- Use descriptive titles for easy searching
- Add detailed descriptions to help students understand the content
- Assign content to multiple classes at once to save time
- Review AI-generated materials and customize if needed

### For Students
- Use the search feature to quickly find specific topics
- Filter by subject to focus on one area
- Download PDFs for offline study
- Complete the AI-generated quizzes to test your knowledge
- Use flashcards for quick review sessions

## Security Notes

- All PDFs are protected by authentication
- Students can only access PDFs assigned to their classes
- Teachers can only manage their own content
- Files are stored securely on the server
- Access is logged for accountability

## Support

If you encounter any issues:
1. Check this guide first
2. Try logging out and back in
3. Clear your browser cache
4. Contact your teacher (students) or administrator (teachers)
5. Report bugs to the development team
